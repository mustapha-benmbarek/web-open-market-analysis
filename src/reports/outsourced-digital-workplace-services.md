# Outsourced Digital Workplace Services

Trends and best practices in the ODWS market find clients demanding a better workplace experience, innovation and value-based deals from their service providers. Gartner has produced a review of 18 providers to help IT sourcing leaders make a selection that matches their desired outcomes.

## Market Definition

Gartner defines the outsourced digital workplace services (ODWS) market as the capabilities required of a provider to deliver consulting, implementation and/or support services to end users who depend on endpoint devices and applications to conduct business.

Services offered by ODWS vendors include integrated and ubiquitous digital workplace (DW)services to increase employee engagement, productivity and digital dexterity in support of the organization’s business strategy.
